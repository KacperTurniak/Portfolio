About Me

I'm a 25-year old CMD student who specializes in 2D and 3D animation. I am most experienced with 3D but I also enjoy working on 2D projects. Below you can read about my experience with software I use most often.

Blender 3D

Although I took a long break from using Blender, I first started using this software during my high school years. I feel very comfortable using it and am familiar with almost all of its shortcuts, which allows me to work efficiently. In addition to being proficient with the software's interface, I am also knowledgeable in the areas of materials, rigs, and animation.

Adobe Animate

While I haven't had extensive experience with 2D animations, I recently had the opportunity to work with Animate and gained a good understanding of how the software functions. Moreover, this experience provided me with an opportunity to learn more about animation principles that are applicable to both 2D and 3D projects.

Adobe Premiere

I have some experience with video editing, including working with Premiere. I am well-versed in many of the software's shortcuts, which allows me to work efficiently. As a result, I typically use Premiere to edit most of my animations.
