Kringlooplandbouw Animation

Another school project in cooperation with CBS, where we worked on a multimedia story about circular agriculture. I worked mainly on the UX and the 2D animations which I made using Adobe Animate. From this project I learned mainly two things about animation. First of which is the usefullness of animation is this kind of projects, because of its ability to illustrate things that may be too costly or impossible to illustrate otherwise. The second thing i learned is how translate complex or confusing concepts to understandable visuals and sound.
